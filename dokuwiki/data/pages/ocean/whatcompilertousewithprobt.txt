Reported to work:
* gcc-2.95
* gcc-2.96 (although this version is generally full of bugs, you should avoid it)
* gcc-3.2 (heavily tested)
* gcc-3.3.3 (probably still lightly tested)

Reported *not* to work:
* gcc-3.3.1, gcc-3.3.2 (random segfaults)

I (RonanLeHy) have just found an instance of a program crashing (very mysteriously) with gcc-3.2, which runs fine with gcc-3.3.3. YMMV.
I (Francis) have also suffered a segmentation fault with gcc-2.96 which disappeared with gcc-3.2, /je dis ca, je dis rien/.
